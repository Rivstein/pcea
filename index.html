Welcome to PCEA Nyahururu Church

Welcome to the Presbyterian Church of East Africa, Nyahururu Parish website. Thank you for finding time to visit us. Kindly take a moment to navigate through this website to have a little glimpse of who we are and what our mission is. Briefly, PCEA Nyahururu Parish exists, like any other Church the world over, to be a faithful representative of Jesus Christ. We believe in the apostolic succession through which we derive our identity.

OUR MISSION STATEMENT
To empower the community which is moulded by the word of God to be change agent, characterized by Godly lives serventhood and integrity.
OUR VISION
A transformed congregation effectively responding to spiritual and physical needs of the community.
OUR CORE VALUES
I - Integrity
M - Meritocracy
A - Accuracy
S - Servanthood
T - Team work
