Diamond Platnumz, most searched musicians in Africa
Evans Mutunga
4th Jan, 2021

Tanzania’s Diamond Platnumz ranked top in the most searched musicians in the country, followed by American rapper Nicki Minaj. Kenyan musicians who appeared on the list included Willy Paul (4) and Khaligraph Jones (10).

The other musicians on the list were Rose Muhando (3), Vybz Cartel (5), Lil Wayne (6), Bahati Bukuku (7), Cardi B (8), and Ali Kiba (9).

Data showed Kenyans were mainly interested in sports-related topics in the general category, with the English Premier League (EPL) ranking first and Sportpesa coming in second.

Locally, the most searched personalities mainly included politicians taking up the top six positions out of 10.

Media personality Betty Kyalo could not hide her surprise when she ranked seven. Taking to her Instagram, the social media sensation said,” I honestly appreciate the interest. Goodness, number 7. The truth is I have been through many highs and lows in life, having started my media career so young. I have gone through most of it in the public eye which is never easy. It is actually quite tough. It is like living your youth plus young adult life with everyone watching.”
